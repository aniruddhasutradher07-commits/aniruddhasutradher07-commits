## 🚀 About Me

I'm a first-year B.Tech Biotechnology undergraduate with a strong pull toward **software engineering, AI/ML, and full-stack product development**. In the span of a single semester, I've shipped three internship deliverables — an NLP chatbot, a sentiment analysis dashboard, and cross-platform Flutter apps — while also studying ML pipelines for biodegradability prediction and disease classification.

I bring an engineering mindset to biology-adjacent problems, aiming to bridge computational tools with real healthcare and life-science applications.
